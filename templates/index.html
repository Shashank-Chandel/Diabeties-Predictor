Diabetes Prediction

How to Measure Inputs

  • Pregnancies: Total number of times you've been pregnant.
  • Glucose: Fasting glucose level via glucometer (mg/dL).
  • Blood Pressure: Use a BP monitor, in a relaxed state (mm Hg).
  • Skin Thickness: Triceps skinfold thickness (optional if unknown).
  • Insulin: Fasting serum insulin level from lab (mu U/mL).
  • BMI: Use BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)2.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: Estimate family history effect (default: 0.5).
  • Age: Enter your age in years.
